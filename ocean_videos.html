Gulf Stream trajectories

Particles started in January 2000 and advected using TRACMASS. Velocities are geostrophic velocities from mean absolute dynamic topography from daily AVISO satellite altimetry. Background colours show absolute velocity.

Gulf Stream trajectories in the North Atlantic

A cluster of particles initiated on 1 Jan 1990 outside the North American east coast and traced for 15 years. Computed using TRACMASS with NEMO data (ORCA025). Each particle is colored according to its current temperature. Created using NCAR Graphics, ImageMagick Convert, and FFmpeg.

RCO SST

Sea surface temperature in the RCO model (Meier et al., 2003) plotted for the years 1962-1969.
