Geostrophic adjustment

A discontinuity in h introduced to a system at rest. Plotted is h(x) in meters with the x-axis scaled by the Rossby radius. Model setup is similar to that of chapter 3.8.2 in Vallis (2006) and chapter 7.6 in Holton (2004).

Kelvin waves

A discontinuity in h introduced to a system at rest. Plotted is h(x,y) in meters with the horizontal axes in kilometers.

Rossby waves

Coriolis parameter is set using the beta-plane approximation. Model is initialised by an eddy in geostrophic balance. A sponge zone is applied in the 10 southernmost and 10 northernmost grid points. Plotted is h(x,y) in meters with horizontal axes in kilometers.

Gyre circulation

System initially at rest, but with an easterly wind stress applied to the south and westerly to the north. Also used is the Laplace diffusion added to the momentum equations, as done by Munk (1950).
