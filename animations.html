Global NEMO simulations at varying horizontal resolution

Simulations done by Andrew Coward at NOC, Southampton.

1/12 degree ("eddy-resolving")

Gulf Stream trajectories

Particles started in January 2000 and advected using TRACMASS. Velocities are geostrophic velocities from mean absolute dynamic topography from daily AVISO satellite altimetry. Background colours show absolute velocity.

Plotted using Matplotlib, Basemap, and Adapter for Mac

Agulhas trajectories

Particles started at all 75 levels in January 2000 and advected using TRACMASS. Velocities are from a global 1/12 deg NEMO simulation. Background colours show model bathymetry.

Plotted using Matplotlib, Basemap, and animation by Adapter.
